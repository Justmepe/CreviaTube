## 🎯 Getting Started

### Creating Your Account

1. **Visit CreviaTube**: Go to [creviatube.com](https://creviatube.com)
2. **Sign Up**: Click "Get Started" and fill in your details
3. **Verify Email**: Check your email and click the verification link
4. **Complete Profile**: Add your social media accounts and payment information

### First Steps

1. **Choose Your Role**: Select whether you're a Creator or Clipper
2. **Connect Social Media**: Link your YouTube, TikTok, Instagram, or other platforms
3. **Set Up Payment**: Add your preferred payment method
4. **Explore Dashboard**: Familiarize yourself with the main features

## 👥 User Roles

### Creator
**Creators** are content creators who want to monetize their content through broker partnerships.

**What Creators Can Do:**
- Create and manage campaigns
- Generate personalized broker links
- Track performance analytics
- Manage earnings and withdrawals
- Access advanced analytics

### Clipper
**Clippers** are users who help promote content and earn commissions.

**What Clippers Can Do:**
- Browse available campaigns
- Generate affiliate links
- Track earnings and performance
- Access clipper marketplace
- View analytics dashboard

### Admin
**Admins** manage the platform and have access to all features.

**What Admins Can Do:**
- Manage all users and campaigns
- Monitor platform performance
- Handle withdrawal requests
- Access system analytics
- Manage platform settings

## 🎬 Campaign Management

### Creating a Campaign

1. **Navigate to Campaigns**: Click "Campaigns" in the main menu
2. **Create New**: Click "Create Campaign" button
3. **Fill Details**:
   - Campaign title
   - Description
   - Target audience
   - Budget
   - Duration
4. **Set Goals**: Define your campaign objectives
5. **Launch**: Review and launch your campaign

- View real-time analytics

- Maintain campaign data

## ❓ FAQ

### General Questions

**Q: What is CreviaTube?**
A: CreviaTube is an advanced content creator platform that helps creators monetize their content through broker partnerships and affiliate marketing.

**Q: Is CreviaTube free to use?**
A: Yes, CreviaTube is free to join. You only pay fees on successful transactions.

**Q: How do I earn money on CreviaTube?**
A: You earn money by promoting broker products and services through your personalized affiliate links.

### Account & Security

**Q: How do I reset my password?**
A: Click "Forgot Password" on the login page and follow the instructions sent to your email.

**Q: Is my data secure?**
A: Yes, we use industry-standard encryption and security measures to protect your data.

**Q: Can I have multiple accounts?**
A: No, each user should have only one account. Multiple accounts may result in suspension.

### Payments & Withdrawals

**Q: How often can I withdraw my earnings?**
A: You can withdraw your earnings once you reach the minimum withdrawal amount ($50).

**Q: What payment methods are supported?**
A: We support PayPal, bank transfers, and various local payment methods depending on your region.

**Q: How long do withdrawals take?**
A: Withdrawals typically process within 1-3 business days.

### Campaigns & Performance

**Q: How do I create a successful campaign?**
A: Focus on your target audience, create engaging content, and track your performance to optimize results.

**Q: Can I edit a campaign after launching?**
A: Yes, you can edit most campaign settings, but some changes may require approval.

**Q: How do I track my campaign performance?**
A: Use the analytics dashboard to monitor clicks, conversions, and earnings in real-time.

### Technical Support

**Q: The website is loading slowly. What should I do?**
A: Try clearing your browser cache, check your internet connection, or contact support.

**Q: I'm having trouble with the mobile app.**
A: Make sure you have the latest version and try reinstalling the app.

**Q: How do I report a bug?**
A: Contact support with details about the issue, including screenshots if possible.
