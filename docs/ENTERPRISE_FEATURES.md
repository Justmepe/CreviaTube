# Enterprise White-Label Features

## Overview
Enterprise users pay for premium access and receive white-label functionality, allowing them to use the complete CreviaTube platform under their own branding.

## Enterprise Features

### 1. Custom Branding
- **Company Logo**: Replace CreviaTube branding with enterprise company logo
- **Color Schemes**: Custom primary and secondary colors throughout the platform
- **Custom Domain**: White-label URL (e.g., enterprise.company.com instead of creviatube.com)
- **Branded Communications**: Custom email templates and notifications with enterprise branding

### 2. Complete Platform Access
- **All Creator Types**: Access to trader, influencer, and entrepreneur creator functionalities
- **Advanced Campaign Management**: Full campaign creation, tracking, and management tools
- **Real-time Analytics**: Comprehensive reporting and analytics dashboards
- **Multi-platform Integrations**: Social media, trading, and analytics platform connections

### 3. Enterprise Controls
- **User Management**: Create and manage user accounts within the enterprise environment
- **Custom Payout Settings**: Set custom payout thresholds and commission rates
- **Permission Management**: Control user roles and access levels within the enterprise platform
- **Advanced Fraud Detection**: Enhanced security measures and bot protection

### 4. Priority Support
- **Dedicated Support**: Priority customer support for enterprise clients
- **Custom Onboarding**: Personalized setup and training for enterprise teams
- **Technical Integration**: Assistance with custom integrations and API access

## Enterprise User Flow

1. **Enterprise Subscription**: Company pays for enterprise-level access
2. **White-Label Setup**: CreviaTube team configures custom branding and domain
3. **Platform Access**: Enterprise users access the full CreviaTube platform under their brand
4. **User Management**: Enterprise admins can create and manage their team members
5. **Campaign Creation**: Launch affiliate marketing campaigns using the full platform capabilities
6. **Analytics & Reporting**: Monitor performance using branded analytics dashboards

## Technical Implementation

### Database Schema
- `enterpriseSettings` field in users table stores white-label configuration
- Custom branding settings (logo, colors, domain)
- Platform settings (allowed creator types, custom rates)
- Admin settings (permissions, reporting features)

### Frontend Components
- Dynamic theming based on enterprise settings
- Conditional branding display
- Enterprise-specific navigation and features
- White-label dashboard layouts

### Backend Services
- Enterprise configuration management
- Custom domain routing
- Branded email services
- Enterprise-specific analytics

## Business Model

### Custom Pricing Structure
- Enterprise customers do NOT pay the standard 20% platform fee
- Pricing is negotiated directly with CreviaTube based on volume and requirements
- Custom commission rates and payout structures are configured by CreviaTube team
- Enterprise accounts receive personalized pricing that fits their business model

### Account Setup Process
1. **Enterprise Inquiry**: Company contacts CreviaTube for enterprise pricing
2. **Custom Negotiation**: CreviaTube team works with client to establish custom rates
3. **Account Configuration**: CreviaTube team configures the enterprise account with agreed pricing
4. **Platform Access**: Enterprise customer receives full white-label platform access
5. **Ongoing Support**: Dedicated enterprise support and account management

Enterprise users pay a negotiated subscription fee to access the white-label platform, allowing them to operate their own affiliate marketing platform powered by CreviaTube technology with custom pricing and their own brand identity.
