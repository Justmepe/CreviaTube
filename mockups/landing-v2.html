Why USDC on Base

The second your metrics cross the line, USDC moves. No 30-day wires, no "the finance team is on holiday," no swap fees eating into the bounty. Just dollar-pegged stablecoins on a chain where transactions cost pennies.

Fees

One number. No surprises.

CreviaTube takes a flat 20% of the campaign budget. That's it. No subscription, no hidden cuts on payouts, no swap spreads.

Example: $10,000 campaign
Creator funds 10,000.00 USDC

No fee on top of clipper payouts. The clipper receives exactly what the campaign rules say they earn.

FAQ

Common questions

Do I need to know crypto to use this?+

If you can use Stripe or PayPal, you'll be fine. We use Reown AppKit for wallet connection, which works with MetaMask, Coinbase Wallet, or you can sign in with email and we'll create a wallet for you.

What happens to unused budget?+

If a campaign ends without hitting its goal, the unspent escrow returns to your wallet automatically. The smart contract enforces it.

Which platforms do you track?+

TikTok, YouTube Shorts, Instagram Reels, X, and Twitch. All verified through their official APIs plus our anti-bot layer.

When do clippers get paid?+

Once verified metrics cross the campaign goal, the contract releases the bounty. Most clippers see USDC in their wallet within minutes of approval.

Is this legal where I am?+

USDC payouts to a self-custodied wallet are available in 100+ countries. We don't currently serve OFAC-sanctioned regions. Check our Terms for the full list.
